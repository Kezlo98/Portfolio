Carousel Draft

AI carousel backgrounds, straight into your TikTok drafts.

Building in public TikTok Content Posting API Drafts, not autopost

Describe a vibe, get a swipe-ready set of background images, and Carousel Draft drops them into your TikTok Inbox as a draft. You add the text overlay by hand on your phone, then post — no scheduling, no auto-publish, you stay in control.

TikTok permissions — in plain English

user.info.basic
Read your TikTok profile (open_id, name, avatar) so we know who you are.
video.upload
The key one — share images into your TikTok Inbox as a draft for you to finish. Nothing posts automatically.
video.publish
Optional, for a future "post now" toggle. Off by default.

Heads-up on carousels: TikTok's API supports photo posts, but photo uploads must be hosted on a verified domain (URL-based), and uploading photos as drafts is still being verified at runtime. If a photo draft doesn't stick, Carousel Draft falls back to a short slideshow video through TikTok's well-documented draft flow — your manual text-overlay step still works either way.

Status

Registering the TikTok app and requesting scope review (typically 5–15 business days). Until the review passes, TikTok restricts uploaded content to private viewing. Follow along on the journey page.
